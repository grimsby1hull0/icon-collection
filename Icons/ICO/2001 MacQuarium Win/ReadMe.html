From the Artist

July 01, 2001

MacQuarium by Kate England

Summer Of X and below Whilst many Mac users are slowly shifting over to OSX there are still many who use the classic operating system. Some of us are happy with our old Colour Classics or Performas, some are enthusiastically unpacking their brand new iBooks, iMacs or G4s and others are rebuilding their old computers – into aquariums.

MacQuarium lets you experiment with the idea of installing fish into your Mac without actually having to go through with the project. Or, as the case may be, in celebration of your MacTank. It lets you paste both fish and the latest hardware on your desktop. It carries vintage models and the newest machines. Both Discus and Guppy. Hopefully MacQuarium makes you feel a little dreamy.

At any rate, you will not have to install OSX to view this fishy set of icons.

Kate

These icons are dedicated to everyone who is excited about the Mac!
