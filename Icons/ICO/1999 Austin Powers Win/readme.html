From the Artist

April 1st, 1999 (April Fools Day!)

The Austin Powers Icon Collection by The Iconfactory

Since its release in 1997, the original Austin Powers movie has obtained cult status. Fans from around the world know and love the movie, the characters and the most die-hard of the bunch can probably quote you Dr. Evil’s entire “Childhood” group therapy speech. Its the kind of movie you either love or hate, and the factory workers fall hard into the “love it” category.

With all the broo-ha-ha over Star Wars: Episode 1 coming this spring, many people have overlooked the upcoming AP sequel, Austin Powers 2: The Spy Who Shagged Me. In order for us to ring in this highly anticipated movie from New Line Cinema, we decided to pay homage to the original movie in all its “randy” glory. The set stands as some of our best work. Every one of the 40 icons was drawn by hand from the pixel up, and some of the icons will probably make you laugh (even if its just on the inside) every time you look at them.

The enclosed collection contains 40 icons from the original 1997 release of New Line Cinema’s Austin Powers™: International Man of Mystery. Characters and objects, all in Copland (3D) style are presented for your free desktop enjoyment. A cast of characters including Austin, Vanessa, Basil Exposition, Dr. Evil, Mr. Bigglesworth & many more bring you back to the swinging era of the 60’s. Hilarious desktop objects from the set include the Swedish Penis Enlarger, Big Boy, the Vulcan Drill and Austin’s dental floss and hip roadster.

Huge thanks go out to Mike Myers, Jay Roach and New Line Cinema for making such a “smashing” cult hit as Austin Powers: International Man of Mystery. The film is brilliant in its own right and we’ve all watched it dozens of times. It never stops making us laugh, and for that, we wanted to pay tribute to you and your hard work. Well done!

Enjoy!
