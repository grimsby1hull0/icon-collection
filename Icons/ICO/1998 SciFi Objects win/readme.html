From the Artist

July 1st, 1998

Sci-Fi Objects by The Iconfactory

Introduction: This icon set represents a great deal to all of us here at the Iconfactory. Not only were these icons a labor of love (we each grew up loving various Sci-Fi movies and TV shows), but the Sci-Fi Objects collection was the catalyst for bringing our friend Dave Brasgalla into the Iconfactory. In March of 1998 Dave had written to Ged to suggest we collaborate on an icon set of some kind. We put off development until we could come up with a cool enough idea for the tandem development. That idea was the Sci-Fi Objects collection. Creation grew to include our favorite characters, ships and gadgets from memorable movies and TV shows, all in the Copland style.

The enclosed folder contains over a hundred Copland style icons based on popular Science Fiction movies and television shows like Tron, Battlestar Galactica, Thunderbirds, Logan’s Run, Bladerunner and Space:1999. We’ve done our best to create a large number of varied icons for this first volume. We know there will be people who will ask why their favorite ship/character wasn’t included, but that’s why we’ve got an email address, right? Look for more of your favorite Sci-Fi icons in more volumes to come. For now, we hope this collection sparks some fond memories as well as some quick trips to the local video store. Have fun!
